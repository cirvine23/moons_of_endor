Motus Vita Est

Welcome to Chefrec!

Chefrec is an app that connects travelers, who are looking to experience a city through food, with the people who are actually making the food. Get recommendations from people who don't just think they know the restaurant scene they ARE the restaurant scene.

With Chefrec you will have access at your fingertips to where the top up and coming chefs in a particular city dine.

Chefrec is the app that will change how you explore cities through food.
